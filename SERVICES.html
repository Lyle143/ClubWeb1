SERVICES

Training Sessions

  • Organized group practices for popular competitive games.
  • Focus on improving teamwork, communication, and in-game decision-making.
  • Run on a fixed schedule to build consistency and discipline.

Coaching And Mentorship

  • Experienced players or alumni guide new and intermediate members.
  • Personalized tips for improving skills, strategies, and reflexes.
  • Encourages a supportive learning environment.

Scrimmage Matches

  • Friendly but competitive matches against other school clubs or local teams.
  • Simulates real tournament pressure without the high stakes.
  • Helps teams adapt to different playstyles and strategies.

Performance Analysts

  • Recorded gameplay is reviewed to spot strengths and areas for improvement.
  • Provides tactical feedback on positioning, timing, and teamwork.
  • Helps players track their progress over time.
